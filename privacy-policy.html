Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users.

Data Collection

No personal information is collected. The app does not require an account, does not track you, and does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

The app may store your saved recipes, recent recipes, and ingredient selections locally on your device. This data is never shared with us or any third party.

Permissions

The app does not request any dangerous permissions that access personal data.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the feedback option in the app.
